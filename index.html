"Where Next?" was created to inspire people to travel and see the world. That includes not just famous towns and cities, but also places of natural beauty such as the Amazon Rainforest or The Scandinavian Mountains.

The world is full of history, cultures and beauty, our mission is to get people outside, exploring and seeing all these different things. We achieve this by allowing users the ability to read about destinations and find out why these places are of interest to them.

You can then further develop your interest in a location by clicking the links to wikipedia articles about the features that location presents. Of course not all of the destinations features are listed on this site, only the most important or iconic ones.

If you are set on going to a location, enter your nearest airport to check the flights and travel arrangements available to you. Be sure to change the type of ticket, the number of passengers and whether the travel is direct or not on skyscanner.net!

Please not the recommendations algorithm was created by myself using weights of my own opinion, and I've only been to two of these destinations. Therefore, I have made my own judgement on if this location are acurate to the questions answered.
